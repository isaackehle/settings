# <img src="https://github.com/kiwiforgmail.png" width="24" style="vertical-align: middle; border-radius: 4px;" /> Email

Email client app for Gmail and Google Workspace accounts.

## Installation

```shell
brew install --cask kiwi-for-gmail
```

## Configuration

Sign in with your Gmail or Google Workspace account.

## Start / Usage

Start: Open the app from Applications.
