## Obsidian Vault

## Job Search

When the user mentions job search, companies, interviews, resumes, cover letters, career, or anything related to job hunting — all output goes into the vault:

- Company files: `$OBSIDIAN_VAULT/job_search/companies/[CompanyName]/`
- Resumes: `$OBSIDIAN_VAULT/job_search/companies/[CompanyName]/`
- Reference resumes: `$OBSIDIAN_VAULT/job_search/resume/reference/` (Full, Web, General variants)
- Templates: `$OBSIDIAN_VAULT/job_search/templates/`

Use absolute paths. Do not write job search files to the current working directory.
